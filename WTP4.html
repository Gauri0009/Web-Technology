Located in the Sadhu Vaswani Mission premises, the Darshan Museum tells the life story and the contributions of Sadhu Vaswani, a visionary, poet and famous educator. This museum is home to the biggest holographic installation in India. An amalgamation of history and technology, the Darshan Museum is a fantastic way of learning about Sadhu Vaswani and his teachings through audiovisual mediums. It lies in the premises of the Sadhu Vaswani Mission, which is visited by people from all around the globe for spiritual orientation and kirtans that are held regularly. Sadhu was also known for women empowerment and alternative education, and was a promoter of spirituality, peace and oneness.
